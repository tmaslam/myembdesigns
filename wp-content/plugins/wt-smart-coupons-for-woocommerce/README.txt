### How to Create Coupon Code in WooCommerce? ###

1. Go to **Smart Coupons** in your WordPress dashboard and click **Add Coupon**.
2. Enter your **coupon code** along with a short description for internal reference.
3. Choose the **coupon type** and define the discount amount.
4. Configure the required conditions under **Usage Restrictions**.
5. Click **Publish** to activate the coupon.

= What Is a First Order Discount? =

A first order discount is a promotional offer available only to customers making their first purchase in your store. It is commonly used to encourage new users to complete their initial order. In WooCommerce, this can be configured by creating a coupon and restricting it to first-time customers using conditions such as user role, purchase history, or specific email restrictions through an advanced WooCommerce coupon plugin.

= How to Disable Coupons in WooCommerce? =

To disable coupons across your entire store:

1. Go to **WooCommerce → Settings → General**.  
2. Uncheck **Enable the use of coupon codes**.  
3. Click **Save changes**.

To disable a specific coupon:

1. Go to **Smart Coupons**.  
2. Choose the coupon you want to disable.  
3. Change its status to **Draft**, or delete it.
